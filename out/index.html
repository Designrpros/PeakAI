faq

What is PeakAI?

An open source AI coding agent that runs entirely on your machine. No subscriptions, no data collection.

How does it work?

Install it, connect your preferred LLM, and start chatting with your codebase.

Is it really free?

Yes. 100% free and open source under the MIT license.

Which models can I use?

Any model that supports the OpenAI-compatible API, including Ollama, GPT-4, Claude, and more.
